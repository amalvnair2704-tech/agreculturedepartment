Soil Type

Brown Soil

Brown soils are a broad group with moderate fertility and variable textures. Local climate and parent material strongly influence their properties.

Key Properties

  • Texture: loam to clay loam
  • Fertility: moderate
  • Organic matter: moderate (varies)

Suitable Crops (Examples)

  • Paddy (where irrigated), vegetables, pulses, coconut (site-specific)

Management Tips

  • Soil test-based fertilizer application
  • Maintain organic carbon using compost/green manure
