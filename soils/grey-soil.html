Soil Type

Grey Soil

Grey soils can occur under specific drainage and parent material conditions. They may need management for structure and nutrient availability.

Key Properties

  • Texture: variable
  • May have drainage/structure issues in some places

Management Tips

  • Increase organic matter
  • Ensure proper drainage where needed
