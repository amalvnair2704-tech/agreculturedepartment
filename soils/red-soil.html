Soil Type

Red Soil

Red soils generally form from weathering of crystalline rocks and may appear red due to iron oxides. Texture ranges from sandy loam to loam.

Key Properties

  • Texture: sandy loam / loam (varies by location)
  • Drainage: usually good
  • Fertility: moderate; may need organic matter and balanced fertilization

Suitable Crops (Examples)

  • Millets, groundnut, pulses, cotton (depending on local conditions)

Management Tips

  • Add FYM/compost to improve structure and water holding
  • Use soil testing for NPK and micronutrients
  • Mulching to reduce moisture loss
