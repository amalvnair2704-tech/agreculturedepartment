Soil Type

Mixed Soil (Soil Complexes)

Mixed soils represent areas where more than one soil type occurs together in a complex pattern. Management depends on the dominant soil within each field.

How to Use This Category

  • Use local soil testing and field observation
  • Manage water and nutrients field-by-field
