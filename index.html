District-wise Soil Proportion

To get soil % by district accurately, intersect Soil Polygons × District Boundaries in QGIS and export a JSON table.

  1. Load district boundary GeoJSON/Shapefile
  2. Load soil polygons (vector) or exported polygons from a WMS
  3. Run Intersection, compute area (km²), and convert to %
  4. Export results to data/district_soil_proportion.json
